About This Project

Smart Hospital No-Show Prediction & Management System — a full-stack ML application that predicts whether a patient will attend or miss a scheduled hospital appointment.

Problem Statement

No-shows are a persistent problem in healthcare: patients book appointments and simply don't come, wasting staff time and blocking slots other patients could have used. Hospitals typically only discover a no-show after the appointment time has already passed.

Solution

MediPredict trains a machine learning classifier on historical appointment data and predicts, before the appointment, the probability a given patient will miss it — based on demographics, chronic conditions, SMS reminders, and waiting time. Hospital staff can then prioritize follow-up for high-risk patients.

Dataset

The model is trained on the public Kaggle "Medical Appointment No Shows" dataset — over 110,000 real outpatient appointment records from Brazil, including patient age, gender, chronic conditions (hypertension, diabetes, alcoholism, handicap), scholarship status, SMS reminder status, and the gap between booking and appointment dates.
