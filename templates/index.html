About the Project

Turning Appointment Data Into Actionable Insight

Missed appointments cost hospitals staff time, block slots that other patients could have used, and disrupt care schedules. MediPredict analyzes patient and appointment attributes — age, gender, chronic conditions, SMS reminders, and waiting time — to estimate the probability that a given appointment will be missed, giving hospital staff a chance to act early.

  • Real historical dataset (110,000+ Brazilian outpatient appointments)
  • Four ML models trained and automatically compared
  • Full admin dashboard with analytics and history

Under the Hood

The Machine Learning Workflow

1
Data Cleaning

Fix column typos, parse dates, remove invalid ages & duplicates.

2
Feature Engineering

3
Model Training

Train Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest & Gradient Boosting.

4
Evaluation

Compare Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1 & ROC-AUC to pick the best model.

5
Deployment

Best model saved with joblib and served live through Flask.
